About Us

Summit Climbing Club is open to anyone who wants to join. We organize social climbing events, trips, courses and youth training. We also keep track of local access issues and make sure we are allowed to climb on all our cliffs. We strive to maintain bolts and anchors in our area.

You don't need to have any experience to join, climbing is a fun sport for everyone!

Climbing is flexible - our sessions are drop-in - you don't need to come to every single one or for the full amount of session time.

Check out our social media pages to keep updated with the latest information and to find out about the upcoming events.
